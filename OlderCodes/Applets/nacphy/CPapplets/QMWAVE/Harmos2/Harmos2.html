Quantum Harmonic Oscillator
Schrödinger equation is solved for a gaussian wavepacket in an harmonic oscillator potential.

The potential and the motion of the wavepacket are plotted at the same time.
    Instructions:
  • Start by pressing button "Start".
  • With the horizontal Scroll bar you can select the wavepacket momentum.
  • With the vertical Scroll bar you can change the potencial strenght.
  • Pressing "Stop", the motion ceases.
