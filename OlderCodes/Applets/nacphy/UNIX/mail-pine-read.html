§ 12.B.2: Reading Mail with Pine

To read your mail, you quit pine and then get back into it:

    Q
    Y
    > pine
    > I
Note that in the last line we entered a I to view the in folder Index.

Move the highlight bar until it covers the message you wish to read. Press [return].

After reading your message, go ahead and delete it with a D while in the message:

A highlighted message appears at the bottom of the screen and tells you Last message deleted. Now you can quit:

    Y
Note that Pine prompts you to make certain you wish to delete your message. Go ahead and delete it:
    Y
