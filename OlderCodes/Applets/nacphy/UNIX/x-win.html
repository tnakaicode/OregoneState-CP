§ 15:  Looking Through X-Windows

The X-Windows environment can be an easy place to work which adapts to your preferences and working style. With a little knowledge, you can learn to use the features of X-Windows and make Unix more transparent. "A Scientist's and Engineer's Guide to Workstations and Supercomputers" has some excellent advice on how to make the most of your X-Windows environment. When you are done with the guide, the next section has instructions on how to change some features of your X-Windows environment.
