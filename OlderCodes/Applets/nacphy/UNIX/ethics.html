§ 4.B: Ethics

All users of the Department Cluster must read, sign, and submit an application form to a member of the Administrative Group. There are important procedures described on that application form which must be followed.

You should also read the OSU Acceptable Use Policy. This document contains further conditions and legal issues related to your computer accounts.

In particular:

  • There is an obligation on the part of those using these facilities and services to respect the intellectual and access rights of others, locally, nationally and internationally.
  • Computing resources and facilities of Oregon State University shall be used for legitimate University instructional, research, administrative, public service, or approved contract purposes. Individuals who disregard elements of this policy will be subject to appropriate disciplinary and/or legal action by Oregon State University.
  • These resources are to be used in an appropriate and legitimate manner for the use authorized. Use of University computing facilities for personal or commercial monetary gain must be consistent with state statutes and Board of Higher Education Administrative Rules.
  • Unless you have explicit permission, you have no business looking into or modifying the files of other users or root. (An unlocked door does not make tresspassing legal.)
  • The electronic mail system shall not be used for "broadcasting" of unsolicited mail. The communication system shall not be used for sending of material that is likely to be considered obscene, offensive, or threatening by the recipient of the material.
  • Much of the software and courseware we use is licensed or copyrighted. Individual users are legally responsible for adhering to the terms and conditions under which such software and courseware were obtained and for protecting the rights of developers. Users agree not to make any copies of licensed software or courseware.
  • Feel free to use the Department Cluster for class work, but not for email (we cannot afford the administrative overhead). As a general rule, a UCS or research account (where you can receive your email) is required before we will give out Cluster accounts. Please don't send email to any Physics Cluster machine except graphy.
